I. INSTALLATION

  1. Double-click the alora.exe file and install to your main BG2 directory
  2. Once the install program has finished, it will launce the WeiDU installer program. Simply follow the on-screen instructions in the new DOS window.

Though this mod is made using the WeiDU standard, and *should* be compatible with all other WeiDU-based mods, there is always the possibility for conflicts.  We suggest you install this mod AFTER the official patches, Baldurdash, and TeamBG mods.  If you encounter any problems installing the mod, please our forums.

II. UNINSTALLATION

Uninstalling is simple. Double-click the setup-alora.exe file and enter the given commands for Uninstallation.
Afterwards, you may delete the following files:

  • alora.exe
  • alora.tp2
  • alora.debug
  • and the contents of the "alora" subfolder

IV. FAQS

Q: What do I need to use this MOD?
A: Alora will require Baldur's Gate II: Shadows of Amn and Throne of Bhaal with the latest Bioware/Interplay patches. We also strongly encourage the installation of the Baldurdash Fixpack as well.

Q: What does this mod do?
A: This mod introduces Alora from the first Baldur's Gate game. She banters with your character and the other game's npcs. She will interject with her environment as well.

Q: Will this MOD add new quests or encounters to the Throne of Bhaal expansion?
A: Nothing at the moment. There may be in a future release.

Q: Why did you decide to develop this MOD?
A: Well because I like Alora and the developers didn't put her into BG2. So if you want something done, you have to do it yourself.

V. GENERAL OVERVIEW

Alora is a WeiDU-based mod for Baldur's Gate II: Shadows of Amn and Throne of Bhaal.  As usual, Alora should be compatible with most other WeiDu-based mods, and should be installed after all official patches and preferably the Baldurdash Fixpack.  Alora requires the Throne of Bhaal expansion pack.
